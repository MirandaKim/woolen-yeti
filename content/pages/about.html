Hi, I'm Miranda...

...and I love to create. By day I'm a web developer, and at night I crochet--often by computer light. My mom taught me to crochet when I was young, and after a long hiatus (or two) it has become an obsession. Now I have more yarn filling boxes and falling off shelves than I know what to do with.

I'm a self-professed nerd and pluviophile, born and raised in the Pacific North West.
